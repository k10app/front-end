Welcome to the K10 Store!

Welcome to the demo app store. To get start using the app, please take the following steps

  1. Go to register to make yourself an account
  2. Head over to the store to buy some items (add them to the basket)
  3. Finish your order by going to checkout.
  4. Once you've completed your order, you can verify your orders in the relevant section or create a new order by going to the store and buy more

If you feel like hacking the catalog, head over to the unsecured admin portal
